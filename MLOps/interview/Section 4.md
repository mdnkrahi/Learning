# Section 4


## **1. Neural Network kya hai aur Activation Function ka kya kaam hai**

Neural Network jaise tumhara dimaag hota hai. Isme bahut saare chhote-chhote cells (neurons) hote hain jo ek dusre se baat karte hain. Jab tum koi cheez dekhte ho to ye cells decide karte hain ki wo kya hai.  

Activation Function ek switch jaisa hota hai. Ye decide karta hai ki cell ko signal aage bhejna hai ya nahi. Jaise tum sochte ho “agar ye laal aur gol hai to seb hai”. Ye switch on-off karta hai.


---

## **2. Backpropagation aur Gradient Descent kya hain**

Backpropagation ka matlab hai peeche se galti ko sudharna. Jaise main tumhara homework check karti hoon, galti dikhaati hoon aur tum sudhaarte ho. Neural network bhi aisa hi karta hai – output galat aaya to peeche jaakar har cell ki galti theek karta hai.  

Gradient Descent ek rasta hai jisse hum sabse neeche (kam galti) tak pahunchte hain. Jaise tum pahadi par se neeche aane ke liye sabse chhote raste ko dhundte ho.


---

## **3. Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) kya hai**

CNN photo dekhne ke liye hota hai. Jaise tum photo mein pehle chhoti-chhoti lines aur edges dekhte ho, phir shapes, phir poori cheez samajhte ho. CNN bhi aisa hi karta hai – pehle edges, phir shapes, phir poori photo ko pehchanta hai.  

Isliye ye photo, chehra, ya kisi bhi cheez ki tasveer samajhne mein bahut achha hota hai.


---


## **4. Recurrent Neural Network (RNN), LSTM aur GRU kya hain**

RNN wo cheez yaad rakh sakta hai jo pehle hui thi. Jaise tum story sun rahe ho – pehle wali baat yaad rakhni padti hai warna end mein samajh nahi aayegi. RNN aisa hi karta hai.  

LSTM aur GRU iske behtar versions hain. Ye lambi story bhi yaad rakh sakte hain. Jaise tum 10 minute ki story sun rahe ho aur end tak sab yaad rehta hai.


---


## **5. Transformer architecture kya hai**

Transformer ek naya tareeka hai jo poori baat ek saath dekhta hai. Jaise class mein sab bachche ek saath baat kar rahe hain bina line mein khade hue. Har cheez har cheez se directly baat kar sakti hai.  

Isliye ye bahut tezi se aur achhe se samajh pata hai. Isne language samajhne aur naye sentences banane mein bahut badi क्रांति laa di hai.


---



## **6. Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) kya hain**

GAN mein do dost hote hain. Ek dost nayi cheez banata hai (jaise nakli photo), dusra dost check karta hai ki wo asli hai ya nakli. Dono ek dusre se seekhte hain. Nakli banane wala behtar hota jaata hai aur pakadne wala bhi behtar hota jaata hai.  

Training mein thodi mushkil hoti hai kyunki dono ko saath mein seekhna padta hai.


---


## **7. Variational Autoencoders (VAEs) kya hain**

VAE bhi autoencoder jaisa hai lekin ye nayi cheezein bhi bana sakta hai. Jaise tum photo compress karte ho phir usse nayi photo bana sakte ho.  

Normal autoencoder sirf copy karta hai. VAE naye examples bana sakta hai jo pehle kabhi nahi dekhe gaye the.


---



## **8. Attention Mechanism kya hai**

Attention ka matlab hai dhyan dena. Jaise tum story sun rahe ho aur koi important baat aati hai to tum uspar zyada dhyan dete ho. Transformer mein har word har word par dhyan deta hai.  

Self-attention mein har cheez khud apne aap se aur dusron se baat karti hai taaki important cheez samajh aa jaaye.

---



## **9. Transfer Learning kya hai**

Transfer Learning ka matlab hai pehle se seekhi hui baat ko naye kaam mein use karna. Jaise tum cycle chalana achhe se seekh chuke ho. Ab jab tum bike chalana seekhoge to cycle wali baat kaam aayegi.  

Deep learning mein bhi ek bada model pehle se seekh leta hai phir hum usko chhote kaam ke liye use kar lete hain. Bahut time aur data bach jaata hai.



---



## **10. TensorFlow aur PyTorch mein kya farak hai**

TensorFlow aur PyTorch dono deep learning ke tools hain. Jaise do alag-alag drawing boxes hain jisme tum pictures bana sakte ho.  

PyTorch thoda aasaan aur flexible hai jaise tum free hand drawing kar rahe ho. TensorFlow bada aur strong hai jab bahut badi machine par kaam karna ho. Dono ka kaam ek hi hai lekin feel alag hai.
